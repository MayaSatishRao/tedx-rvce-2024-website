RESONATING CHORDS

Like the resonant notes of a perfectly tuned instrument, we beleive that when we come together, our collective voices can create a symphony of inspiration and change. Join us on a journey of harmonious ideas, profound connections, and inspiring voices. Let's strike the perfect chord together!

SPEAKERS

Saakshar Duggal

Artificial Intelligence and Law Expert

Saakshar Duggal is a remarkable personality in India, known for his expertise in the legal aspects of Emerging Technologies such as Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain. He has been recognized as an Artificial Intelligence Law Expert and has delivered TEDx talks on ten occasions. In addition, he has been invited as a guest speaker at important organisations such as the United Nations main office (UN), Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), and more. Saakshar is dedicated to raising awareness of legal issues in cyberspace and upcoming technologies such as Artificial Intelligence. His ambition is to make India Cyber Resilient.

Manasi Gupta

Mental Health Advocate

Manasi Gupta is a social entrepreneur and an engineer by profession. At the age of nineteen, she founded HuesOfTheMind Foundation, a UN SDSN and YOUNGO member nonprofit organisation to provide mental health services which have impacted 50,000+ beneficiaries, reaching more than 1.2 million viewers in the past four years. She has been awarded the Diana Legacy Award and conferred nationally for her indelible social impact. This is also reflected in the multitude of nominations and representations that she has been entrusted with including at the University of Oxford, United Nations, the Forbes Under 30 conference, and the annual Harvard conference. Put succinctly, she is a mental health advocate with the vision to make mental health resources more accessible, affordable, and available. She believes that courage, consistency and empathy are the biggest catalysts of change.

Shyam Ramaswami

Cyber Threat Hunter

In a world where digital threats lurk around every corner, one man along with many cyber warriors stands as the guardian of cyberspace. Meet Shyam Sundar Ramaswami, a cybersecurity virtuoso with 16 years of battle-hardened experience. As the Senior Staff Cybersecurity Architect at GE Cyber Labs, he's the frontline combatant against the darkest forces of the digital underworld. Shyam's expertise spans both offense and defense, navigating the treacherous terrain of cyber warfare. With two TEDx talks under his belt, Shyam captivates audiences with his insights into the ever-evolving cyber landscape. His book, "It's Your Digital Life," is a manifesto for the modern age, empowering individuals to take control of their online presence. But his mission doesn't end there – armed with a master's degree in digital forensics, he imparts his wisdom as a cybersecurity educator, shaping the defenders of tomorrow, to secure our digital future, one line of code at a time.

Ashwini Neetan

Maternity and Newborn Photographer

Ashwini Neetan is the visionary artist behind Tiny Yawns Photography, specialising in the tender moments of maternity and newborn photography. With an innate ability to capture the delicate beauty of new life, Ashwini brings a unique blend of creativity and compassion to every session. Drawing inspiration from the profound connection between mother and child, her work encapsulates the joy, anticipation, and sheer wonder of this precious time. Through Tiny Yawns Photography, Ashwini invites expecting parents to embark on a journey of timeless memories, preserving the fleeting moments of pregnancy and the tender innocence of newborns in stunning detail. With each click of the shutter, Ashwini crafts images that serve as cherished mementos, forever capturing the love and magic of this extraordinary chapter in life. She started off as a travel photographer 13 years ago and somewhere in the middle, babies caught her attention and she heard her calling! From being featured in multiple newspapers and magazines to getting felicitated by CM Siddaramaiah in 2017 in Vidhana Soudha to recently being recognised by Nikon, a leader in the world of cameras as an ‘Official Nikon Expertive/Creator’ – the path she chose and the way she pursued it, gave her a purpose and made it all meaningful. And her purpose being – to work hard everyday, enthusiastically – to meet newborn babies and pregnant mommas to give them memories that last through generations!

Shobha Rani

Panchayat Development Officer

Dr. Shobha Rani, a distinguished Panchayat Development Officer, has left an indelible mark on the villages she's served. Beginning her impactful journey as the Chief Officer of Malangi Panchayat, she later assumed leadership roles in Harohalli and Nagawala before currently heading Periyapattana. Driven by her passion for grassroots development, she has spearheaded transformative policies in education, waste management, and more, significantly enhancing the quality of life for residents. Her exceptional contributions have garnered widespread recognition, earning her prestigious accolades including the Kayaka Ratna Award, Gandhi Gram Award, Niramla Puraskar, Karnataka Mahila Ratna Award, and the Deccan Herald Changemakers Award. With her profound understanding of administration and unwavering commitment to community welfare, Dr. Shobha Rani continues to champion initiatives aimed at fostering holistic development at the grassroots level, thereby paving the way for the advancement of the nation as a whole.

Rohith Saraswathi

Storyteller

A full-time corporate engineer turned into a full-time professional STORYTELLER. His hitchhiking trip to Mt. Everest changed his life in more than one too many ways. He has travelled the whole country on his motorcycle to augment awareness about chasing dreams. He has inspired more than 1.5 lakhs of students by visiting 200+ different educational institutions conducting storytelling sessions for free of cost across Karnataka. He is recipient of “Bharatiya Samskruthi Bhushana” from “The Yoga University of America”

Fostered by the vision of serving the student community with the most innovative and inspiring ideas.
